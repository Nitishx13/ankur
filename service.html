Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the sourcing process typically take?

The timeline varies based on product complexity and market conditions, but initial supplier identification typically takes 2-4 weeks.

What happens if a quality inspection fails?

If an inspection fails, we immediately halt production/shipping and coordinate remediation with the manufacturer at their expense, keeping you informed at every step.

Do you handle international customs and duties?

Yes, our Door-to-Door services include managing all customs clearance procedures, duties, and taxes based on the agreed Incoterms.
